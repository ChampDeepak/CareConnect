Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

CareConnect system ("Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform to book medical appointments.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email, phone number)
  • Appointment details (date, time, medical concerns)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type)

3. Use of Information

Your data is used to:

  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Send appointment reminders
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with:

  • Authorized medical practitioners
  • Payment processors (for paid services)
  • Legal authorities when required

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls
